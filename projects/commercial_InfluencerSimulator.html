Influencer Simulator

Available on itch.io

With the request from Huawei to help promote their developer's toolkit. I made a influencer simulator game that integrates their toolkit functions.


I've always wanted to make an influencer simulator and when Huawei wanted to sponsor the video for promoting their developers toolkit, I felt like it was time that I start making this simulator. I've added a lot of functions to this game, from playing in-game games, recording videos, editing videos, uploading them, to gaining comments and subscribers, earning money, and purchasing different items for your home. I've designed a whole new UI interface for a Lindows 10 operating system, integrated Sleam gaming platform, Laobao online shopping, Lr video editing tool, LBS video recording tool, and Lililili video platfrom to the game.

A complete process of uploading a video includes, purchasing a game from Sleam platform, recording your gameplay video and editing it, and then uploading. There are scripts for managing your videos, subscribers and such. It was fun putting all these together and be able to simulate a video platform where you could actually grow as an influencer, which is what most of my subscribers wanted to experience. So the game got over 300 thousand downloads.

I've made 4 mini games inside this game, namely, Lario platformer, Lota 2, CL:GO and Initial L. I've learned a lot about game data save and load system and how json serialization works during the development of this project.
