Dream Star

Available on itch.io

With the request from Tencent to help promote their game Dream Star, I made my own version of the game Dream Star, adding new characters to the game.


Tencent launched a new title party game called Dream Star, and wanted me to help promote it by making a game. I found the idea of putting my other game Arrow a Row's character into it, as a colab, would be interesting. So I went about and modeled three game characters, with two of them having special skills. I wasn't provided with any models this time. So I modeled everything that there is in this game.

Since the time-span of making such sponsered videos aren't normally long and I have to go through the process of communicating with the sponsor, making the game, making the video and modifying things in basically one month, I didn't put too much efforts on the fidelity of the models. But I was happy with the result.


The character AI was implemented using NavMesh from Unity. I made all the obstacles into a single prefab that can be toggled to its different variation, so the development process was rather smooth. Most efforts were put into modeling in this game.
