Cross Fire

Available on itch.io

With the request from Tencent to help promote their game CrossFire: Legends, I made a new version of the game Cross Fire, adding new game modes.


The request for this game was to make two game modes, one associated with cooking, and the other with barber. These are some of the new game modes Tencent was adding to their game CrossFire: Legends.

So I went about adding some spoons, chef hats, and pot of food to the game. And the game mechanics is that the player shoots the enemy and tries to steal the food from the enemies' pot using their bowls. I don't normally do many FPS games, so the AI in this game ended up a bit sloppy and the shooting was a bit off. But efforts were more put into the game mechanicss such as stealing food and shaving others bald. Most of the models were provided by Tencent so I didn't do much modeling.


I am really satisfied with how the mobile UI came out. I composed them in photoshop myself and figured what would be the best scale and position to put each icon and buttons. The game works both in Windows and Android. The mini-map was also a fun thing to implement.
