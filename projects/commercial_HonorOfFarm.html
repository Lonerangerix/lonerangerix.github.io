Honor of Farm

Available on itch.io

With the request from Tencent to help promote their game Honor of Kings, I made a new version of the game by myself, suited to their needs.


This is a sponsored project from Tencent. I was provided with a rough map and a character model with some animation. But I guess they surely weren't the original ones in their actual Honor of Kings game. I put together some UI replicating the Honor of Kings style and added touchscreen support as well.

I've added a farm to the game, that the player needs to defend from the enemies and farm crops. The crops were online assets and I put some time implementing the growing function and a UI indicator on top of each crop. It was indeed satisfying to watch the crop grows and the UI becomes "harvestable".

Funnily enough, I actually put lots of efforts on optimizaing the game for this project. And before this, I wasn't putting too much attention to optimization because those were just small games. This time around, I felt like learning more about optimization, so I tried to decrease the draw calls, compress the textures, and ended up cutting the Android apk's size from 230MB to 90MB, which would be easier for people who want to download and try.
