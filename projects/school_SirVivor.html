Sir Vivor

Available on itch.io

Sir Vivor is a survival game made for the game design course in a team of 5 people. Game mechanics, coding and UI design were made by me.


This is a project where I acted as a project manager whilst developing the game as well. I would say I did a better job in developing than project managing. I didn't assign the work to the team that well, and did lots of stuff on my own. But my teammates were great and they took upon the modeling and music composing.

During this project, I realized the importance of laying the game framework well, and make it scalable and flexible, on which future work can be built on. I did made lots of functions decoupled and utilized ScriptableObjects from Unity but I guess I could do better in the future. It was a valuable experience. Game Design Document can be seen below.
