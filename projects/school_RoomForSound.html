Room For Sound

Room For Sound is a vr game that lets you play different instruments while being able to interact with real audiences who join via android phones.


Room For Sound is one the my proudest projects during my master study. The project was displayed both at ForskarFredag and Tekniska Museet and received lots of positive feedback. It is a VR game where you could get your hands on various kinds of instruments and be able to interact with audiences who joined via their android phone.


It was implemented by setting up a web sever that would receive data from android phones who scanned the QR code we provided, which includes nicknames and messages. Then the data was fetched to Unity using web socket and instantiated audience models under the stage. The characters are able to display messages and make different moves such as dancing, clapping hands, etc.
