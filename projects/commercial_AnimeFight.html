Anime Fight

With the request from Tencent to help promote their game Naruto Mobile, I made a anime fight game that includes multiple characters.


Tencent wanted to promote their new game character Senju Hashirama for their Naruto mobile game. After some discussions, we decided to make a ragdoll fighting game that includes other anime characters as well. I modeled the character and did 3 ultimates animations for the character, matching their design.

But this is actually my first time learning ragdoll physics, so I spent some time figuring out how to make the joints move with physics but also follow a certain animation. Damage detection was implemented by checking the speed at which the sword hits the enemy. The overall effect came out fine.
