Space War

Available on itch.io

With the request from bilibili to help make a video for their New Year's Eve Party, I made a video about developing a space war game which were streamed during the New Year's Eve Party, to more than 100 million people.


I was provided with a specific theme for this sponsorship from bilibili, "sending gifts to the whole universe". That was the theme for a part of the show from bilibili's New Year's Eve Party. For designing the game, I took it literal and made spaceships fight for gathering more gifts in space. I pre-downloaded all of my subscribers' ID and made them randomly appear as an AI ship in the game.

The show was perceived pretty well and we had a nice new year that year.
