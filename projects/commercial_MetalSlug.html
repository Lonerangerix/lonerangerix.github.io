Metal Slug 3D

Available on itch.io

With the request from Tencent to help promote their game Metal Slug Awakening, I made a 3D version of the original Metal Slug game by myself.


Since Tencent's new Metal Slug game was a 3D one, I wanted to promote it by integrating a first person 3D metal slug in my game. I modeled the game characters, environment and with the gun models I was provided with, I was able to put together a 3D version of the first level from Metal Slug. In this project, I put extra efforts on making the game look nicer, since it is supposed to be a stylized game instead of a realistic one. So I messed around with lighting and post processing to get this look for the game.


Modeling the environment was a lot of work and I did my best. Guess I need to enhance my blender skills to replicate this kind of scenes better.
