Object Detection in VR

This is my bacherlor's thesis project. It utilizes Unreal Engine for traffic simulation while detecting objects in real-time using yolo.


The project was developed as proof of concept for real-time image detection in simulated traffic. The main goal was to spot special vehicles from the traffic and let auto-driving cars make way for them. It involved compling the UE4 with Carla, and integrating python image detection into the engine. I implemented a camera onto the auto-driving car which would capture its view and send the picture to yolo detection each frame, and receive the detected data back. This was my first time dealing with UE4 but thanks to my knowledge in blender and Unity, the whole process went rather smooth. And I was also able to learn about CNN and trained models that would recognize special vehicles.

The thesis got a high score of 95 out of 100 as an excellent graduation project.
